# Employee Leave Policy 2026

## Annual leave
Full-time employees receive 24 days of annual leave per calendar year.

## Planned leave
Planned leave should be requested at least 10 business days before the first day of leave. Approval depends on staffing and business needs and cannot be guaranteed.

## Carry-forward
Up to 5 unused annual-leave days may be carried into the following calendar year.
